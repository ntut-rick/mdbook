## 8.11

$$ 
\begin{aligned}\\
\text{ F-measure}=&\frac{2}{\frac{1}{precision}+\frac{1}{recall}}\\
&=\frac{2}{\frac{TP+FP}{TP}+\frac{TP+FN}{TP}}\\
&=\frac{2}{\frac{2TP+FP+FN}{TP}}\\
&=\frac{2TP}{2TP+FP+TN}\\
\end{aligned}\\
$$
